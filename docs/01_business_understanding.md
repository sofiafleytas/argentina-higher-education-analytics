# Business Understanding

## Project

Argentina Higher Education Analytics

## Background

Higher education plays a critical role in the social and economic development of Argentina.

Understanding enrollment, graduation trends, institutional growth, and the participation of different academic areas can provide valuable insights for educational planning and policy making.

This project aims to analyze the evolution of higher education in Argentina between 2015 and 2025 using publicly available datasets.



## Business Problem

Educational stakeholders need a clear understanding of:

* How student enrollment has evolved.
* How graduation rates have changed.
* Differences between public and private institutions.
* The participation and growth of STEM-related disciplines.
* Regional and institutional trends.



## Stakeholders

### Government and Educational Authorities

Interested in understanding the evolution of the higher education system.

### Universities

Interested in benchmarking enrollment and graduation trends.

### Researchers and Analysts

Interested in identifying educational patterns.

### Students

Interested in understanding educational opportunities and trends.



## Main Business Question

How has higher education evolved in Argentina between 2015 and 2025?



## Secondary Questions

1. Has student enrollment increased over time?
2. Have graduates increased at the same rate?
3. What academic areas have the largest participation?
4. How important are STEM disciplines?
5. What differences exist between public and private institutions?
6. What impact did the COVID-19 pandemic have on the educational system?



## Project Scope

The analysis focuses on:

* Higher education institutions.
* Student enrollment.
* Graduates.
* Academic disciplines.
* Public and private institutions.
* National trends between 2015 and 2025.
